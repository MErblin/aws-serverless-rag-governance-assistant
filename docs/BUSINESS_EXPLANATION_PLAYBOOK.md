# Business Explanation Playbook (Non-Technical)

Use this when speaking to business stakeholders about the platform.

## One-liner
This platform turns company documents into reliable AI assistants for different teams, so people get accurate answers faster without searching through files.

## What it does for a business
- Saves time spent searching manuals, policies, contracts, and SOPs
- Improves consistency of answers across teams
- Reduces risk by grounding answers in approved documents
- Supports multiple departments with isolated assistants (Support, Billing, HR, etc.)
- Provides traceability (citations + audit trail)

## How multi-team setup works (simple)
Think of each team as having its own private assistant:
- Support assistant -> support documents only
- Billing assistant -> billing documents only
- Compliance assistant -> policy documents only

Each assistant has its own:
- document library
- search index
- behavior/rules
- logs/audit history

This prevents cross-mixing answers between teams.

## Business outcomes to highlight
- Faster onboarding and support operations
- Lower manual review time
- Better response quality and confidence
- Easier compliance and accountability

## 60-second sales version
"We give each team a private AI assistant trained only on that team’s documents. Staff ask questions in plain English and get grounded answers with sources. That cuts search time, improves consistency, and keeps knowledge usable across support, billing, compliance, and other workflows."

## Best practice for documentation style
For every feature, document in 4 blocks:
1. **Business Value** (why it matters)
2. **User Impact** (what changes for staff/customers)
3. **Control/Risk** (how safety/governance is handled)
4. **Proof** (which metric shows improvement)
